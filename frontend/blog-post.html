Idea Title

  • By : Student name

  • 35

The graduation project is a type of evaluation for the senior project to test their knowledge of the labor market. This has been used in the university and high schools for many years. However, at the beginning of the project, the students faced a problem with how to build it, so they needed instructions for building it or an example of the Senior Project. Therefore, building a website that contains instructions and models for students will make it easier for them to build their graduation project. Technological advancement has a huge impact on website building. It provides many new tools and technologies, making the website-building process easier and more efficient..

The proposed solution is to build a portal (website containing previous graduation projects with community member commentary to allow students to learn from previous mistakes or know what is best to produce outputs of higher standards. The proposed solution is the Saudi Electronic University Graduation Projects Portal. It targets the Saudi university members especially the computing and informatics department. The major objectives of these portal are helping the students facilitate graduation projects, enhancing the graduation project quality, helping the students to construct the idea for their project, and reducing the students' tension at the beginning and during building the project.

The used method to build this solution is following the waterfall model to produce a website. The languages used to build it are HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. MySQL will be used to build the database.
